A Few Words About Earth

Year
2100
world situation

The population in 2100 has reached approximately 11,200,000,000 people, with Africa exceeding double its population in 2020. The ocean has increased 0.1 meters due to the rise on Earth's temperature (1.5º C), the cities are becoming now floating islands. Many migrations have taken place due to the risk on the coasts, grouping the population into “micro nations” based on their beliefs and lifestyles. Artificial Intelligence has replaced almost all jobs in the military, political, medical and business fields, as well as contributing to human intelligence.

At the moment you born, an implant is placed in your forehead, through a microsurgery made by specialists, who connect the chip to your cerebral cortex. The chip becomes your backup brain. This implanted chip connects to an interface (bracelet), to give you the possibility to go back and forth in your memories; thanks to biotechnology. It also allows you to add skills in your brain, which you can acquire in a "skill market". The way this new skill information enters to your brain is through a pill; once it enters to your body the chip detects it and send a signal both to the brain, to store this information, and to the interface to control this ability. Likewise, whoever has major skills for some activities, will be able to sell this information from their brain to the “skill market”, to be bought by others. The chip's capacity depends on the price you pay for it, this capacity establishes the skills' quantity and quality you are able to store.
