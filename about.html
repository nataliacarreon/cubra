A Few Words About Earth

Year
2100
world situation

The population in 2100 has reached approximately 11,200,000,000 people, with Africa exceeding double its population in 2020. The ocean has increased 0.1 meters due to the rise on Earth's temperature (1.5º C), the cities are becoming now floating islands.

Many migrations have taken place due to the risk on the coasts, grouping the population into “micro nations” based on their beliefs and lifestyles. Artificial Intelligence has replaced almost all jobs in the military, political, medical and business fields, as well as contributing to human intelligence.
